# 🎧 Affirmation Player

> A modern **Python desktop affirmation & meditation audio player** with streak tracking, waveform visualization, and automatic sleep mode.

Affirmation Player helps users build a **daily mindset habit** by playing affirmation audio, tracking listening sessions, and visualizing playback with an animated waveform. The application also maintains streaks and can automatically put the PC to sleep when affirmations finish.

# ✨ Features

## 🎵 Smart Audio Player
- Load and play **any MP3 affirmation audio**
- Full media controls  
  - ▶ Play  
  - ⏸ Pause  
  - ⏵ Resume  
  - ⏹ Stop  
  - ⏮ Seek backward  
  - ⏭ Seek forward
- Interactive **timeline slider**
- Real-time playback timer

---

## 🌊 Animated Waveform Visualizer
- Dynamic **multi-layer waveform animation**
- Smooth easing transitions
- Particle sparkle effects
- Gradient color bars

---

## 📊 Affirmation Habit Tracking
- Automatically logs every listening session
- Stores:
  - Date
  - Time
  - Duration
  - Audio filename

---

## 📅 Streak & Calendar System
- Weekly **affirmation streak tracker**
- Calendar highlighting completed days
- Encourages consistency in daily practice

---

## 📋 Session History Dashboard
- Scrollable session history
- Filter sessions by **date or audio file**
- Shows **daily listening totals**
- Export history to **CSV**

---

## 🌙 Smart Sleep Mode
When the affirmation finishes:

- The PC **automatically enters sleep mode**

But if playback is **stopped manually**, sleep **will not trigger**.

Perfect for **night affirmations before sleeping**.
